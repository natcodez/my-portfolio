About Me

Hi, I’m Nathaniel Edet, a Cloud & DevOps Engineer with a strong focus on AWS cloud architecture, automation, and security. I design and implement solutions that combine Infrastructure as Code (IaC), CI/CD automation, and Kubernetes orchestration, ensuring systems are both scalable and secure.

While AWS is my core specialty, I also have working knowledge of Azure, Google Cloud, and Oracle Cloud, which allows me to adapt to multi-cloud environments and support organizations with diverse infrastructure needs. My experience in cloud security from IAM and encryption to network design and compliance ensures the solutions I build are not only efficient but also resilient.

Beyond cloud engineering, I have extensive experience as a WordPress Developer, delivering custom websites and e-commerce solutions. This unique blend of skills enables me to bridge the gap between cloud infrastructure and real-world application deployment.

When I’m not working on cloud projects, I enjoy teaching computer fundamentals and empowering others with the skills they need to thrive in the digital world. I’m currently seeking opportunities to contribute to forward-thinking organizations where I can apply my expertise in AWS, DevOps, and security-driven automation to solve complex challenges and drive innovation.
